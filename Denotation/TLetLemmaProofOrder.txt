inside `tlet_intro_denotation` in `Denotation/theories/TLet.v`.

Already proved / no longer pending
----------------------------------

- `value_shift_lc_id`, `tm_shift_lc_id`
  These are already available in the new route and are not blockers.

- `value_lvars_no_bv_of_lc`, `tm_lvars_no_bv_of_lc`
  The current Term layer compiles with the split lstore instantiation route.
  They are no longer part of the active TLet blocker list.

- `lstore_instantiate_open_back_mutual`
  The split lstore/open-back normalization needed by formula/result opening is
  already present enough for the current compilation path.

- `expr_eval_in_atom_store_tlete_elim`,
  `expr_eval_in_atom_store_tlete_intro`,
  `expr_total_on_tlete_elim_e1`
  These wrappers are proved in `Term.v`.  The lower core lemmas are the ones
  used directly.

- `lstore_instantiate_tm_split_restrict_fv`,
  `expr_eval_in_atom_store_restrict_fv_closed_on`
  Proved in `Term.v`.  The second lemma no longer needs global closedness of
  the whole store; it follows from the structural fact that lstore
  instantiation only inspects `fv_tm e`.

- `tlet_over_forall_fv_subset`, `tlet_arrow_forall_fv_subset`
  Both FV transport obligations are now proved at the TLet level.  Arrow uses
  the upper-bound FV helper `formula_fv_denot_ty_lvar_gas_mono_env_or_term`.

- `basic_tm_has_ltype_open_one` (old iff)
  Deleted.  The iff was not needed by TLet and forced an unnecessary alpha
  renaming obligation.  The active interface is cofinite/forward only.

Proof order for remaining work
------------------------------

1. BasicTypingFormula cofinite opening

- `basic_tm_has_ltype_open_one_cofinite`
  Status: provable; prove this first.
  Reuse:
  * `open_tm_env_insert_commute`
  * `ChoiceTypeLanguage/theories/LtyEnvOpen.v`:
    `lty_env_open_lvars_insert_fresh`
  Needed small helper shape:
  * compose arbitrary `η` by switching to `delete k η` and then inserting
    `[k := y]`.
  Proof sketch:
  Choose `L` containing `lvars_fv (dom Σ)`, `fv_tm e`, and the currently visible
  atoms needed by the arbitrary `η`.  Given target `η`, reduce
  `open_tm_env η (open_tm k (vfvar y) e)` and
  `lty_env_open_lvars η (lty_env_open_one k y Σ)` to the original
  `basic_tm_has_ltype Σ e T` obligation under `<[k := y]> (delete k η)`.

- `formula_open_expr_basic_typing_formula`
  Status: should not be proved through a standalone iff.
  Preferred proof:
  Use `logic_qualifier_ext` plus the cofinite/renaming route only if exact
  formula equality remains necessary for `formula_open_denot_ty_lvar_gas`.
  Alternative:
  Replace consumers by a models-level transport lemma and delete this exact
  equality later.

2. Basic world closure support

- `wfworld_closed_on`, `wfworld_closed`
  Status: added and proved support wrappers in
  `ChoiceBasicDenotation/theories/StoreTyping.v`.

- `basic_world_formula_wfworld_closed_on_dom`
  Status: proved.
  Meaning:
  A world satisfying `basic_world_formula Σ` is closed on the atom projection
  of `dom Σ`, not necessarily on its whole domain.

- `basic_world_formula_wfworld_closed_on_atoms`
  Status: proved.
  This is the form that result-extension lemmas should use:
  if `lvars_of_atoms X ⊆ dom Σ`, then the world is closed on `X`.

3. Result extension world models

- `expr_result_extension_world_models_closed`
  Status: proved.
  Statement now uses `wfworld_closed_on (fv_tm e) m`.
  Reuse:
  * `res_extend_by_store_iff`
  * `expr_result_extension_apply_total_iff`
  * `expr_eval_in_atom_store_restrict_fv_closed_on`
  * `expr_total_on_atom_world`
  Proof shape:
  Unfold `expr_result_at_world`.  For a store in `res_lift_free mx`, project to
  an atom store in `mx`, decompose it through `res_extend_by_store_iff`, obtain
  evaluation of `e` from totality on the base store, use `wfworld_closed_on` to
  justify restricting to `fv_tm e`, then identify the singleton output via
  `expr_result_extension_apply_total_iff`.

- `expr_result_extension_world_models`
  Status: proved wrapper.
  It now derives `wfworld_closed_on (fv_tm e) m` from
  `basic_world_formula Σ` and `lvars_of_atoms (fv_tm e) ⊆ dom Σ`.

- `expr_result_formula_of_result_extends`
  Status: thin wrapper after the closed-world lemma.
  Reuse:
  * `expr_result_formula_models_iff`
  * `expr_result_extension_world_models`

4. TLet total/result formula wrappers

- `expr_total_formula_tlete_intro_from_result_extension`
  Status: provable after the operational totality composition is in place.
  Reuse:
  * `expr_total_formula_models_iff`
  * `expr_eval_in_atom_store_tlete_intro`
  * `expr_result_extension_apply_total_iff`
  * `wfworld_closed_on` from the basic-world premise
  Proof sketch:
  For each base store in `m`, totality gives an `e1` result.  The result
  extension turns that into a store in `mx`; body totality gives a result for
  `e2 ^^ x`; `expr_eval_in_atom_store_tlete_intro` combines them.

- `expr_result_at_world_tlete_elim_body_ext`
  Status: provable after `expr_result_extension_world_models_closed`.
  Reuse:
  * `expr_eval_in_atom_store_tlete_elim`
  * `expr_result_extension_apply_total_iff`
  * `res_extend_by_store_iff`
  Proof sketch:
  A result of `tlete e1 e2` in `my` decomposes into an `e1` result plus an
  opened-body result.  The extension `Fx` supplies exactly the additional
  singleton result cell in `mxy`.

- `expr_result_formula_tlete_elim_body_from_result_extension`
  Status: thin wrapper after the at-world lemma.
  Reuse:
  * `expr_result_formula_models_iff`

5. Denotation open/FV support

- `formula_fv_denot_ty_lvar_gas_mono_env_or_term`
  Status: true-looking, but current direct proof should be optimized before
  attempting again.
  Why it exists:
  TLet Arrow needs an upper-bound FV transport where extra FV from `e1` is
  absorbed by the target environment instead of the target term.
  Suggested helper:
  * `fv_tapp_shift_env_or_term`
      `fv_tm e1 ⊆ E ∪ fv_tm e2 ->
       fv_tm (tapp_tm (tm_shift 0 e1) (vbvar 0)) ⊆
       E ∪ fv_tm (tapp_tm (tm_shift 0 e2) (vbvar 0))`.
  Reuse:
  * `fv_tapp_tm`
  * `tm_shift_fv`
  * existing `formula_fv_denot_ty_lvar_gas_mono_env_term` proof shape
  Proof sketch:
  Redo the gas induction, but use the small tapp FV helper in Arrow/Wand
  branches instead of asking `set_solver` to solve the whole expanded goal.

- `formula_open_denot_ty_lvar_gas_typed_bind_current`
  Status: provable, but depends on formula-open component lemmas.
  Reuse:
  * `formula_open_choice_ty_wf_formula`
  * `formula_open_basic_world_formula`
  * `formula_open_expr_basic_typing_formula`
  * `formula_open_expr_total_formula`
  * `formula_open_expr_result_formula`
  * `type_qualifier_formula_open`
  Proof sketch:
  Induct on gas and destruct `τ`; each atom uses its formula-open lemma.
  Arrow/Wand use the typed-bind-current normalizers.

6. TLet Arrow helpers

- `tlet_arrow_arg_extend_obligation`
  Status: provable after FV support cleanup.
  Reuse:
  * `res_models_extend_base_iff`
  * `formula_fv_denot_ty_lvar_gas_subset_env_term`
  * `formula_fv_denot_ty_lvar_gas_insert_fresh_upper`
  Proof sketch:
  The argument denotation does not inspect `x` under the freshness premise, so
  it transports from `my` to `mxy` across `Fx`.

- `tlet_arrow_result_from_body_denotation`
  Status: prove last among named helpers.
  Reuse:
  * `expr_basic_typing_formula_tlete_intro`
  * `expr_total_formula_tlete_intro_from_result_extension`
  * `expr_result_formula_tlete_elim_body_from_result_extension`
  * `formula_open_denot_ty_lvar_gas_typed_bind_current`
  Proof sketch:
  Treat `my` as the base world and `mxy` as the `e1` result-extension world.
  The premise over `mxy` is the opened body denotation needed by the IH for the
  result type.
