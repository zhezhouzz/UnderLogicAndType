# Definition/Lemma Lowering Review

Scope: Denotation, ContextBasicDenotation, ContextTypeLanguage, ContextLogic.

Goal: identify definitions and lemmas that are currently proved too high in
the stack, but can be generalized and lowered to ContextBase, ContextStore,
ContextAlgebra, ContextLogic, ContextTypeLanguage, or ContextBasicDenotation.

Conventions used below:

- "Move" means the statement is already essentially lower-layer and should be
  relocated with only import/update churn.
- "Generalize then move" means the current statement is useful, but is too
  specialized to value/type/lty_env/formula syntax and should be restated with
  a smaller generic interface first.
- "Keep" means the lemma genuinely depends on recursive context-type
  denotation or on TLet-specific proof architecture.

High-level summary
==================

1. The largest immediate lowering opportunity is in
   Denotation/theories/TypeDenote.v.  Its first half defines
   and proves generic formula/term multi-open facts.  Most of those facts do
   not depend on ty_denote_gas and should move down:

   - generic formula_open_env and constructor distribution lemmas -> ContextLogic
   - open_env facts for basic_world/expr_total/expr_result/basic_typing atoms
     -> ContextBasicDenotation
   - pure term open/lvars/shift facts -> CoreLang or ContextBasicDenotation
   - lty_env restrict/relevant-env facts -> ContextTypeLanguage/ContextStore

2. ContextBasicDenotation contains several modules that are partly semantic and
   partly pure syntax/store algebra.  The pure parts should be split downward:

   - TermSyntax.v lines 12-662 are mostly CoreLang syntax/lvars/open facts.
   - TermSyntax.v lines 674-1029 are lstore-instantiation facts and should stay
     BasicDenotation unless ContextStore grows value-aware instantiation.
   - StoreTyping.v currently specializes "store/world satisfies a typing
     context" to ty/value/basic_typing.  A relation-parametric version should
     live in ContextStore; BasicDenotation should specialize it.

3. ContextTypeLanguage still owns several generic lvar-store operations.  Some
   should move to ContextStore's lvar-key store layer:

   - lstore/lty_env shift/open/projection/closedness lemmas that do not mention
     context_ty syntax.
   - the generic map_fold_ext_on_lookup helper should move to ContextBase or
     ContextStore.

4. ContextLogic is mostly at the correct level.  The main downward movement is
   not from Logic, but into Logic: formula_open_env, formula_lvars_at, and
   formula-level open/FV/scoping decompositions currently sitting in Denotation
   should be owned by ContextLogic.

5. There is a larger phase-2 abstraction opportunity: logic_qualifier and
   type_qualifier are structurally the same dependent-domain qualifier pattern,
   differing mainly in the "input family" (LWorldOn vs LStoreOn) and the
   corresponding open-back operation.  A generic dependent-qualifier record
   could remove duplication, but it is a higher-risk refactor and should not be
   mixed into the first lowering pass.

   Denotation/TypeDenote.v to ContextLogic/FormulaSyntax.v.

   Denotation/TypeDenoteLvars.v to ContextLogic/FormulaSyntax.v.

   - basic_world_formula -> ContextBasicDenotation/StoreTyping.v
   - expr_total_formula and expr_result_formula -> ContextBasicDenotation/TermOpen.v
   - expr_basic_typing_formula/context_ty_wf_formula -> ContextBasicDenotation/BasicTypingFormula.v
   - type_qualifier_formula -> ContextBasicDenotation/Qualifier.v or
     ContextTypeLanguage/Qualifier.v depending on import direction.

   ContextTypeLanguage/LtyEnv.v or ContextStore's lvar-store layer.

   order permits.
