## Scope

This report covers Rocq tactics from `ContextBasicDenotation` upward, while
also recording the lower-level shared solvers they depend on.  The inventory was
collected with `rg` over `Ltac` and `Tactic Notation` definitions.

## Integration Conclusions

1. The useful shared solvers are already concentrated in:
   `ContextBase/Tactics.v`, `ContextBase/BaseTactics.v`, and
   `ContextStore/Store.v`.
2. Denotation tactic consolidation should not create another wrapper file unless
   there are real callers.  The previous `TypeDenoteTactics.v` had no callers
   and was deleted.
3. Const proof tactics should stay local because their lemma set is
   const-specific.
4. Old msubst/instantiation Ltac residue in `BasicTypingFormula.v` was removed.
5. Future optimization target: if `FormulaConnectives.v` is refactored again,
   revisit the local model-normalization tactics with zero current external
   use.
