Folder-Local Stdpp Facts / Regular Ltac Optimization
====================================================

Goal
----

Keep pure stdpp reasoning in local set/map solver layers, and add
deterministic "regular" extraction only for repository-specific definitions.
Pure hypotheses such as `x ∉ A ∪ B` or `m !! x = Some v` are not extracted;
they are handled by the folder solver.  Hypotheses such as
`context_typing_wf ...`, `ctx_denote_under ...`, `ty_denote_gas ...`, and
resource-extension predicates may produce derived stdpp facts.

References Checked
------------------

- UnderType/HATs tactic layering:
  1. pure set/map solver (`fast_set_solver`, pruning, map lookup helpers),
  2. regular extraction from project definitions,
  3. high-level proof tactics that run regular extraction, normalization, then
     the pure solver.
- This repo already has the pure solver base in `ContextBase/Tactics.v`.
