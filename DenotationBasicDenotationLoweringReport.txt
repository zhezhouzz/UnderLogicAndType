# Denotation / BasicDenotation lemma lowering report

Scope: this report only inspects `Denotation/theories` and
`ContextBasicDenotation/theories`.  It does not propose proof changes yet.

Classification:
- A: should be generalized and moved down.
- B: should be generalized, with a thin local wrapper only if it keeps the
  upper proof readable.
- C: keep in Denotation/TLet for now; it is genuinely tied to the type
  denotation recursion or to the statement of `tlet_intro_denotation`.

## High-level conclusion

Most of the reusable material currently sitting in `Denotation/theories/TLetSupport.v`
falls into three lower layers:

1. Pure variable/set/open facts: move to `ContextBase` or `ContextTypeLanguage`.
2. Term operational/basic-typing facts: move to `ContextBasicDenotation`.
3. Resource/extension shape facts: move to `ContextAlgebra` or `ContextLogic`.

The largest immediate payoff is to move the `tapp/tlete` spine family out of
`TLetSupport.v`.  Those lemmas are not about `tlet_intro_denotation`; they are
the reusable transport theorem for the syntactic associativity
`tapp_tm (tlete e1 e2) (vfvar y)` vs `tlete e1 (tapp_tm e2 (vfvar y))`.

## Recommended migration order

1. Move pure lvar/set helper lemmas from TLetSupport to `ContextBase` /
   `ContextTypeLanguage`.
2. Move `tapp_tm_fvar_spine` and pure spine syntax lemmas to
   `ContextBasicDenotation/TermSyntax.v`.
3. Move spine basic-typing/eval/total/result lemmas to
   `BasicTypingFormula.v` and `TermTLet.v`.
4. Consolidate `relevant_env_*` lemmas in
   `Denotation/TypeDenote.v`; delete the duplicate
   `relevant_env_dom_subset_direct` in TLetSupport.
5. Create a Denotation associativity file for the `ty_denote_gas`
   `tapp/tlete` transport family.
6. Re-run the scan and remove remaining one-off wrappers from TLetSupport.
