Highest-Value Cleanup Targets
-----------------------------

1. Finish the remaining comma-bind context bridge.

   Target:

   - `denot_ctx_under_bind_from_result_denotation`

   Needed proof structure:

   - Take a fiber of `mx` over `erase_ctx_under Σ Γ`.
   - Commute that fiber with `m #> Fx ~~> mx`, producing the corresponding
     base fiber of `m`.
   - Use `denot_ty_in_ctx_under_fiber_elim_projection_instantiated` or the
     msubst theorem to move from the source denotation of `e1` to an
     instantiated source denotation.
   - Use `denot_ty_lvar_gas_result_extension_to_var` to obtain the denotation
     of `tret (vfvar x)` in the extended context.
   - Package the result as `denot_ctx_under (erase_ctx_under Σ Γ)
     (CtxBind x τ1)`.

   Risk:

   - The extension/fiber commuting lemma may need to be added in Algebra or
     Logic.  It should be general: extension output fresh from the fiber
     domain, projection over a base domain, and `res_extend_by`.

2. Prove `basic_world_formula_fibvars_elim`.

   This should be the dual of the newly proved intro lemma.  It likely belongs
   in `StoreTyping.v`, but the proof should reuse the same msubst/basic-world
   transport facts from `MsubstTransport.v` to avoid unfolding the lqual atom
   twice.

3. Reduce `ContextTypeDenotationMsubstScoped.v`.

   Keep the current semantic split (`Core`, `Guard`, `KeepDomain`, `Scoped`,
   `Compat`) but move repeated local packets into small helpers:

   - store-singleton projection restricted to formula FV under frame/product
   - opened argument/result scope packets
   - repeated atom-store typing after `store_restrict`
   - repeated `formula_open_msubst_store_fresh` + opened denotation
     normalization

   Avoid creating huge helpers whose premises are just the whole local context.
   If a helper statement is more than a few lines, first look for a lower-level
   Logic/Algebra lemma.

4. Clean `TLetSupport.v` by syntax shape, not by TLet-specific shape.

   The repeated patterns are:

   - nested `FAnd` / `FImpl` destruct-intro steps
   - formula FV/open normalization
   - `formula_scoped_in_world` extraction from guard facts
   - resource agreement on formula FV

   These should be moved toward Logic syntax/semantics tactics where possible.
   TLet-specific lemmas should only mention TLet when the operational term
   fact is genuinely about `tlete`.

5. Keep `ContextTypeDenotationCases.v` as the semantic boundary.

   Soundness should call direct case lemmas from this file.  Future cleanup
   should group direct obligations by shape:

   - const/appop finished or near-finished cases
   - Lam/App/Fix/Match direct case obligations
   - disjoint variants (`letd`, `lamd`, `appd`, `fixd`)

   The proof support for each group should be separated from the admitted
   semantic theorem statements.

6. Improve solver coverage for Store/Base casts.

   Many `change` occurrences come from `StoreA`, `World`, `LWorld`, and
   `res_lift_free` casts.  The next solver improvements should target:

   - `dom (store_restrict ...)`
   - `storeA_restrict_lookup_some`
   - `lvars_fv_elem` / `lvars_fv_of_atoms`
   - `res_lift_free_dom`
   - `res_fiber_from_projection_world_dom`

   This would shorten `StoreTyping.v`, `MsubstTransport.v`, and msubst scoped
   proofs without changing statement shapes.


Suggested Batch Order
---------------------

Batch A: finish proof repair.

- Prove the extension/fiber commuting helper.
- Prove `denot_ctx_under_bind_from_result_denotation`.
- Prove or intentionally defer `denot_ty_lvar_gas_result_extension_to_var`.
- Run `make` and commit.

Batch B: close the basic-world fiber pair.

- Prove `basic_world_formula_fibvars_elim`.
- Move shared proof fragments between intro/elim into a small helper if the
  two proofs duplicate more than a few lines.
- Run `make` and commit.

Batch C: msubst scoped cleanup.

- Do not change public msubst theorem statements.
- Replace repeated local assertion packets with short helpers.
- Keep compatibility admits isolated.
- Run `make` and commit.

Batch D: TLetSupport and Cases cleanup.

- Move formula syntax normalization to ContextLogic if it is not Denotation
  specific.
- Keep operational TLet lemmas in BasicDenotation/TermTLet.
- Keep Denotation-level direct case lemmas in Cases.
- Run `make` and commit.

Batch E: Soundness corollaries.

- Once case bridges are clean, restore the corollary proofs or leave them as
  the only Soundness-level named obligations.
