# Direct Soundness Transport Recovery Plan

## Summary

Stop trying to prove the full generic `ty_denote_gas_tm_fiber_equiv`: the
Arrow/Wand cases are too strong for the current nondeterministic result
semantics.  Keep the useful fiber/projected facts for Over/Under reasoning, but
restore the Soundness-facing route through the direct lemmas that are actually
needed.

Allowed temporary admits for this recovery pass:

- `ty_denote_gas_result_alias`
- `tlet_intro_denotation`

All other Denotation/Soundness support should be restored as real proofs when
possible.

## Recovery Order

1. Quarantine the false full fiber transport path.
   - Do not use the full `TypeEquivFiber` theorem as the public transport.
   - Keep `typed_fiber_equiv_on`, `tm_result_equiv_projected_on`, and
     `tm_fiber_equiv_to_projected_on` as local useful facts.
   - Remove false Arrow/Wand app-frame obligations from the active compile path
     if they are only supporting the rejected full theorem.

2. Restore Denotation basics.
   - Restore relevant-env/open/env-agree/fv/scope support in `TypeDenote`.
   - Restore core gas support in `TypeEquivCore`, including saturation,
     fresh insert, typed extension, and result-alias guard.
   - Keep current `expr_result_formula_at` and nondeterministic `CTSum`
     semantics.

3. Restore direct term-equivalence transport.
   - Rebuild the main-style direct `ty_denote_gas_tm_equiv` route.
   - Use direct body helpers only as Soundness support.
   - Do not make direct `tm_equiv_on` the new fiber-transport theorem.

4. Restore Soundness-facing wrappers.
   - Keep `ty_denote_gas_result_alias` as a named admitted theorem with a
     comment.
   - Prove `ty_denote_gas_result_ext` using result extension plus the admitted
     alias.
   - Keep `tlet_intro_denotation` as a named admitted theorem with a comment.
   - Restore restrict/delete/drop-fresh lemmas used by Soundness.

5. Restore Soundness compilation.
   - Recompile from Denotation upward.
   - If a Soundness proof body was replaced by an admitted skeleton, restore
     the closest compatible proof shape from `main`, adapted to the current
     nondeterministic result semantics.

  normalize the opened `expr_result_formula_at`, coarsen it to

  A genuine lower-layer prerequisite for this route is gas monotonicity:
  branch hypotheses provide `ty_denote_gas (S gas) Σ τ e`, while the opened

  use a reusable lemma such as
  `ty_denote_gas_pred : m ⊨ ty_denote_gas (S gas) Σ τ e -> m ⊨ ty_denote_gas gas Σ τ e`

  This is not a timing issue: under the nondeterministic `CTSum` result-graph
  shape, branch denotations plus a global result witness require a precise
  branch/result-alias bridge for pulled-back result worlds.  Do not hide this
  by reviving the rejected full fiber transport theorem.
  The proof route is documented next to the remaining lemma.  The key missing
  lower lemma should combine result-world pullback with
  `expr_result_formula_at_coarsen_domain`, then use the existing admitted
  `ty_denote_gas_result_alias` only as the alias step.
