# Lemma Lowering And Generalization Plan

## Purpose

This optimization pass moves reusable proof facts down to the lowest layer
where their statements make sense.  The goal is to keep Soundness proofs focused
on typing/semantic orchestration, while store, qualifier, resource, syntax,
open/shift, and formula facts live closer to their definitions.

This pass does not delete lemmas.  Existing upper-layer lemmas should be kept as
compatibility wrappers when callers already use their names.

## Skill Guidance Used

- Prefer lower-layer, reusable statements over proof-local store/set chains.
- For dependent records, prove raw carrier facts first and keep dependent
  wrappers thin.
- Generalize repeated `assert`/`pose` patterns into lower-layer lemmas.
- Do not silently confuse subset-style facts with Kripke/projection/fiber facts.

## Layering Rules

- `ContextStore`
  - raw store restriction, insert, lookup, dom, filter/map facts.
- `ContextAlgebra`
  - resource restriction, product, sum, extension, fiber, projection facts.
- `ContextQualifier`
  - qualifier FV/open/msubst/swap/store-level lookup facts.
- `ContextLogic`
  - formula syntax/semantics, `FPersist`, `FFibVars`, `FOver/FUnder`,
    `fiberwise_joinable_on`, connectives.
- `ContextBasicDenotation`
  - term result/total/basic typing atoms and their formula-level wrappers.
- `Denotation`
  - type-denotation facts, result-first type transport, context denotation.
- `ContextTyping`
  - only facts that genuinely require `has_context_type` or
    `context_typing_wf`.

## Concrete Optimization Work

1. Audit each long lemma for non-local facts.
   - If a proof step mentions only store/resource operations, lower it.
   - If a proof step mentions only qualifier/open syntax, lower it.
   - If a proof step mentions formula semantics but no type denotation, move it
     to `ContextLogic`.
   - If a proof step mentions `ty_denote_gas`, keep it in Denotation.

2. Generalize shape-specific helpers.
   - Replace facts specialized to `vfix`, `tapp`, `tlet`, or `CTOver` when the
     proof only needs a generic term/formula/resource shape.
   - Keep the old specialized lemma as a wrapper.

3. Separate raw and dependent facts.
   - First prove raw store/world equality.
   - Then prove a thin `WfWorldT` or `LWorldOn` wrapper.

4. Add compatibility wrappers instead of deleting.
   - Upper-layer theorem names stay stable.
   - Wrappers should be short and documented as compatibility wrappers.
