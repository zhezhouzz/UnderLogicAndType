Method
------

- Ran a clean build with `make clean && make TIMED=1`.
- Computed `real seconds / KLOC` from the TIMED output and source line counts.
- Ignored tiny re-export files as primary optimization targets when their high
  sec/KLOC was just Rocq startup/import overhead.
- For each substantial target, used Rocq `-time` to locate slow sentences before
  editing.
