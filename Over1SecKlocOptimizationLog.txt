# >1s/KLOC Optimization Log

This log tracks optimization batches driven by `CompileTimeStats.txt`.

## Candidate Policy

- Treat every file above `1 s/KLOC` as having possible optimization room.
- Prioritize files that are both high total time and high density.
- Ignore tiny re-export files as primary targets unless they hide avoidable
  imports; their density is dominated by fixed Rocq startup/import overhead.
- Each batch should identify a concrete slow proof pattern, replace it with a
  smaller lemma/tactic/normalization step, compile the affected file, and then
  commit.
