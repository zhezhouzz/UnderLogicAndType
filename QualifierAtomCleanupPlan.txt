# Logic Qualifier / Atom Wrapper Cleanup Plan

## Goal

Remove the old `logic_qualifier` / `lqual_*` route and the
`type_qualifier_formula` wrapper.  After this cleanup, logic atoms use only the
unified store-level `qualifier`:

- `FAtom q` is the exact atom formula.
- `FFiberAtom q` remains definitional sugar for
  `FFibVars (qual_vars q) (FAtom q)`.
- No `logic_qualifier`, `lqual_*`, `LogicQualifierT`,
  `type_qualifier_formula`, or `FQual[ q ]` remains in source.
