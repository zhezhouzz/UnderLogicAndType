Remaining Stdpp / Regular Ltac Optimization Tracker
====================================================

Purpose
-------

Track remaining stdpp-heavy proof patterns after the first folder-local facts
pass.  The goal is not to erase all stdpp from the repository: low-level
Store/Resource files are naturally stdpp-heavy.  The goal is to eliminate
repeated upper-layer proof blocks where project-specific hypotheses can
deterministically produce the required stdpp facts.
